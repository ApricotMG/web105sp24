Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a fighting game that is only avaiable on the Nintendo Switch. You can play with up to 8 players for the most intense battle. This game is the 5th installment in the series

Gameplay

This game has the most amount of characters in any fighting game avaiable. There are currently 82 playable characters in the game that you can choose from. 69 of them are free, and the other 13 are DLC, or paid downloadble content.

My Favorite Characters

There are over 82 fighters to choose from, but my favorite fighter in the game is fighter #70: Piranha Plant. Piranha Plant is the plant villain from the Super Mario series. This character is very wacky that it was added into the game more as a joke. It's not an assist trophy, but an actual fighter!
